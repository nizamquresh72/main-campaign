About the campaign

"The Boy Who Saved an Anniversary" is a heartwarming tale that explores the power of small gestures to strengthen family bonds and create moments of joy. It celebrates thoughtfulness, love, and the quiet ways in which we bring light and happiness into the lives of those we care about. This film also honours our retailers and highlights how HRD Antwerp serves as the silent voice of trust that enables such special moments. The film was adapted into multiple versions to feature the stores of our different retail partners.
